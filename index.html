Ship game

Controls

Front arrow:
Lower sails to speed up.
arrow:
Raise sails to slow down.
Left arrow:
Turn Left.
Right arrow:
Turn right.
Space Bar:
Shoot cannon on both sides of ship. Reload time is 2.5 seconds. Every cannonball deals 1 damage with a chance of dealing extra when hitting ships on the front or back.
Ram:
not a button, but i didnt know where else to put this. Colliding with enemy ships deals 1 damage to both ships and knocks both ships back.
Health:
player has 40 health that does not recover with each level

Enemies


Frigates:
 blue ships that are the same shape and have the same weapons as the player (they shoot faster tho). have 10 health.
Gunboats:
 small green boats with a front cannon that aims at the player. have only 1 health but can be difficult to hit.
Galleons:
 The Largest and strongest enemies you will face. its Red and has 3 cannons on either side but are slower and turn slower than other ships. Have 20 health. Ramming is not recommended.

Other info

Levels:
There are 5 levels in the game.
Ramming:
Colliding with enemy ships deals 1 damage to both ships and knocks both ships back.
Health:
player has 40 health that does not recover with each level
